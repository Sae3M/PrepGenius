## 📝 What is PrepGenius?

PrepGenius is a tool designed to help students and job seekers get ready for interviews. It uses AI to check how well your resume matches a job opening. It also creates practice interview questions and helps you improve your resume to pass automated screening systems. This toolkit is made to guide you step-by-step, even if you don't have technical skills.

## 🔧 How to Use PrepGenius

PrepGenius has three main features designed to help you prepare for interviews.

---

### 1. Resume-Job Match Analysis

- Upload your resume and the job description.
- The tool checks how well your resume fits the job.
- It shows you where to improve your resume to increase the match.

---

### 2. Mock Interview Questions

- PrepGenius generates questions similar to ones you might face.
- Pick topics or skills you want to practice.
- Use these questions to build confidence and prepare answers.

---

### 3. ATS Score Improvement

- Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) scan resumes before they reach a recruiter.
- PrepGenius helps you optimize your resume keywords and format.
- Your resume will have a better chance to pass the ATS filters.
